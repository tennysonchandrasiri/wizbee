The Goose That Laid the Golden Eggs

Story

Once upon a time, a farmer discovered that one of his geese had laid a shiny, golden egg.

Farmer (picking it up): “This is real gold! I’m rich!”

The next morning, the same goose laid another golden egg. And again the next day.

Farmer (excited): “One golden egg a day! Soon I’ll be the wealthiest man in the village.”

For weeks, the farmer sold the eggs and grew richer. But his excitement slowly turned into impatience.

Farmer (thinking): “Why should I wait for one egg a day? The gold must be inside the goose. If I cut her open, I can have it all at once!”

Without thinking further, he killed the goose. But when he looked inside, there was no gold — only what you’d find in any ordinary goose.

Farmer (shocked): “What have I done? My goose… my golden eggs… all gone!”

He had destroyed the source of his wealth out of greed and impatience.

Moral: Greed can destroy the very thing that brings you good fortune.
