About Wizbee English School

"The eye to see the world through"

Empowering young minds across Sri Lanka with engaging, interactive English language education that opens doors to global opportunities.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality English language education to children across Sri Lanka, fostering communication skills, cultural understanding, and confidence that will serve them throughout their lives.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital platform for English language learning in Sri Lanka, where every child, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to develop strong English skills through innovative and engaging educational experiences.

Our Story

Wizbee English School was born from a collaboration between dedicated educators at the Regional English Support Center in Ampara and the Ministry of Education Sri Lanka. Recognizing the critical importance of English language skills in today's interconnected world, we set out to create an innovative digital platform that would make quality English education accessible to all Sri Lankan children.

Funded by the General Education Modernization Project, our platform represents a significant step forward in educational technology for Sri Lanka. We combine traditional pedagogical excellence with modern interactive technology to create an engaging learning environment that children love.

Today, Wizbee English School serves thousands of young learners across the country, helping them build the language skills they need to succeed in school, pursue higher education, and eventually contribute to Sri Lanka's growing presence in the global economy.
