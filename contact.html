Frequently Asked Questions

Is Wizbee English School free to use?

Yes! All our educational content is completely free and accessible to everyone.

Can I use these materials in my classroom?

Absolutely! Educators are encouraged to use our materials for non-commercial educational purposes.

Do I need to create an account?

No registration is required. You can access all content directly from our website.
